# Honor of Kings Tier List (June 2026): Every Role Ranked S to D

*by Marcus Tran · Updated June 2026 · 12 min read*

---

This tier list is based on the current Global Server meta as of June 2026. Rankings factor in win rate, pick rate, ban rate, and — most importantly — how these heroes actually perform in ranked games from Diamond to Mythic.

A quick note before we dive in: tier lists are a snapshot, not gospel. A B-tier hero piloted by someone with 500 games on them will beat an S-tier hero played by someone who just unlocked them. Use this as a guide, not a rulebook.

---

## How the Tiers Work

**S Tier:** Dominant in the current meta. High pick/ban priority. These heroes define how the game is played.

**A Tier:** Strong, reliable picks. Not ban-worthy, but always a good choice.

**B Tier:** Viable but situational. They work in the right comp or as counterpicks.

**C Tier:** Niche picks. You can win with them, but you're working harder than you need to.

**D Tier:** Avoid in ranked unless you're a one-trick.

## Key Meta Observations (June 2026)

1. **Jungle is the most impactful role.** S-tier junglers (Fatih, Lam, Musashi, Sun Ce) decide games. If you want to climb, learn one of them.

2. **Da Qiao is the best hero in the game.** She's S-tier in both Mid and Support. Her global teleport wins games at every rank. She's banned in most high-elo games.

3. **Marksmen are in a decent spot.** No S-tier marksmen right now, but the A-tier ones (Arli, Marco Polo, Consort Yu) are all viable and have clear strengths.

4. **Mulan is the best blind-pick Clash Laner.** She has no truly unplayable matchups and can adapt her playstyle to any situation.

5. **Donghuang is the best counterpick in the game.** He shuts down every mobile assassin and diver. If the enemy team has Fatih, Lam, Li Bai, or Han Xin, lock Donghuang and they can't play the game.

---

## Final Thoughts

This meta is defined by jungle dominance and utility supports. If you're a jungler, learn Fatih or Musashi. If you're a support, learn Da Qiao or Donghuang. These four heroes will win you more games than any others in June 2026.

The tier list will shift with patches. We'll update this monthly.
