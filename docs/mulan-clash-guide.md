# Mulan Guide: The Most Versatile Fighter in Honor of Kings

*by Marcus Tran · Updated June 2026 · 10 min read*

---

Mulan is the fighter that does everything. She has damage reduction, CC immunity, a silence, burst damage, sustained damage, and two completely different forms she can swap between. She can dive backlines, peel for carries, split push, and frontline — sometimes all in the same game.

She's also hard to play well. Her skill ceiling is up there with Li Bai and Han Xin. But if you're willing to learn her, she's one of the most rewarding heroes in the game.

---

## Skills Breakdown

### Passive: Blade Waltz

Mulan has two forms: Light Sword (fast attacks, mobility) and Heavy Sword (slow, high-damage attacks). She can swap between them with her ultimate.

In Light Sword form, her skills apply silence stacks. At 5 stacks, the enemy is silenced and slowed. This is your setup tool.

In Heavy Sword form, her skills deal bonus damage and she takes reduced damage while casting. This is your execute form.

### Skill 1 (Light): Sky Slash

Mulan dashes forward and slashes twice. If she hits an enemy, she can dash again. Two charges.

This is your gap closer and escape in Light form. Use the first dash to engage, the second to chase or reposition.

### Skill 1 (Heavy): Unyielding Slash

Mulan charges up and slams her sword down in a line. Deals massive damage in an area. She takes reduced damage while charging.

This is your main damage ability. It hits hard and the AoE is big. Use it to clear waves, zone enemies, and execute low-HP targets.

### Skill 2 (Light): Spinning Blade

Mulan throws a spinning blade that damages enemies in a line and stays on the ground. Enemies in the blade take damage over time. Mulan can pick up the blade by walking over it, reducing the cooldown.

Use this to poke, zone, and apply silence stacks. The cooldown reduction when you pick it up means you can throw it again almost immediately.

### Skill 2 (Heavy): Relentless Charge

Mulan dashes forward, pushing enemies along with her and dealing damage.

This is a short dash but it's a displacement tool — you can push enemies into your team, away from your carries, or into a wall for a stun.

### Skill 3 (Ultimate): Form Swap

Swaps between Light and Heavy Sword forms. Also deals AoE damage around Mulan in both forms.

This is what makes Mulan Mulan. You can swap forms mid-combo, which means you have six skills instead of three. The swap itself also deals damage, so use it aggressively.

---

## The Combo

Standard engage:

**Light form → Skill 1 (dash in) → auto → Skill 2 (throw blade) → auto → Skill 1 (second dash) → Ultimate (swap to Heavy) → Skill 2 (push) → Skill 1 (charge slam) → Ultimate (swap back to Light)**

That's eight inputs. It's a lot. But once the muscle memory is there, you can execute this in about two seconds and kill almost any squishy from full HP.

## When to Pick Mulan

Mulan fits into almost any comp. She's a good blind pick because she can adapt her playstyle — play aggressive into weak laners, play safe into strong ones.

She struggles against:
- Guan Yu (out-kites you)
- Heavy CC comps that lock her down before she can swap forms
- Ranged Clash laners that can poke you from safety

---

## Quick Takeaways

- Learn the full combo in practice tool before taking her to ranked
- Light form for mobility and poke, Heavy form for damage and execution
- The silence from your Light form passive wins duels — stack it fast
- You have damage reduction in Heavy form. Use it to tank burst.
