# How to Play Around Your ADC: A Support and Jungle Coordination Guide

*by Marcus Tran · Updated June 2026 · 9 min read*

---

Your ADC (marksman) is your team's primary source of sustained damage. They take towers, they kill objectives, and in the late game, they're the reason you win fights. But they're also the squishiest member of your team and the number one target for every enemy assassin.

If you're a support or jungler, your job is to enable your ADC. Here's how.

---

## For Supports: Your ADC Is Your Responsibility

### Laning Phase

The first five minutes of the game are the most important for your ADC. If they fall behind early, they're useless for the next 15 minutes.

1. **Provide vision.** Ward the river bush and the enemy jungle entrance. Your ADC can't farm if they're scared of a gank.

2. **Don't take their farm.** Last-hit nothing. Your gold comes from your support item, not from minions.

3. **Harass the enemy laner.** Poke them when they go for last-hits. Make them choose between farming and taking damage.

4. **Know when to roam.** If your ADC is safe (wave is pushed, enemy is backed), roam mid or help your jungler. But don't abandon your ADC for three minutes while they get dove under tower.

### Team Fight Phase

1. **Stand between your ADC and the enemy.** You're the wall. If the enemy assassin wants your ADC, they have to go through you first.

2. **Save your CC for divers.** Don't waste your stun on the enemy tank. Save it for Fatih, Lam, or whoever is trying to kill your ADC.

3. **Use your items.** If you built a shield item (Crimson Shadow - Radiance), use it on your ADC, not yourself. If you built a heal item, heal your ADC first.

4. **Die for your ADC if you have to.** A dead support and a living ADC wins more fights than a living support and a dead ADC.

---

## For Junglers: Gank Your ADC's Lane

### Early Game

Too many junglers ignore the Farm Lane entirely, focusing on mid and Clash. This is a mistake. A fed ADC wins games.

1. **Gank Farm Lane at level 4.** Coordinate with your support. Wait for the enemy to push up, then collapse.

2. **Counter-gank when the enemy jungler shows.** If you see the enemy jungler on the minimap heading toward Farm Lane, rotate immediately. A successful counter-gank can turn into a double kill and a tower.

3. **Don't tax the wave.** If your gank is successful, help push the wave to the enemy tower so your ADC can recall. But don't take the farm yourself.

### Mid to Late Game

1. **Peel for your ADC in team fights.** You don't always have to dive the enemy backline. Sometimes the best play is to stay near your ADC and kill whoever tries to dive them.

2. **Give your ADC the red buff.** In the mid to late game, the red buff is more valuable on your ADC than on you. The slow and burn damage help them kite and deal more sustained damage.

3. **Secure objectives near Farm Lane.** Dragon is the most important early-game objective, and it's right next to Farm Lane. Your ADC should be there for every Dragon fight.

---

## For ADCs: How to Make Your Team Want to Help You

A quick note for ADC players reading this: your team wants to help you. But you need to be helpable.

1. **Don't push without vision.** If you're auto-attacking the enemy tower with no wards and no idea where the jungler is, you're not "applying pressure." You're feeding.

2. **Show up to objectives.** If your team is fighting for Dragon and you're farming bot lane, you're throwing. Be there.

3. **Ping when you need help.** Your support can't read your mind. If you're getting dove and need help, ping.

4. **Don't flame your support.** They're trying. Probably.

---

## Quick Takeaways

- Supports: provide vision, don't take farm, save CC for divers, die for your ADC if needed
- Junglers: gank Farm Lane early, counter-gank, give red buff to ADC late game
- ADCs: don't push without vision, show up to objectives, ping when you need help
