# Arcana System Explained: How to Set Up Your Arcana Correctly

*by Marcus Tran · Updated June 2026 · 8 min read*

---

Arcana are Honor of Kings' version of runes — passive stat bonuses you equip before the game starts. A full arcana page gives you the equivalent of a free item's worth of stats at level 1. That's huge.

Here's how the arcana system works and how to set yours up without wasting resources.

---

## What Are Arcana?

Arcana are small stat boosts that you equip in three categories: Red, Blue, and Green. Each category has 10 slots (30 total). You unlock slots as your account levels up.

Each arcana gives a small bonus — a few points of attack damage, a percentage of attack speed, some armor penetration. Individually they're tiny. Together, a full page of level-appropriate arcana gives you a significant early-game advantage.

---

## The Three Arcana Types

### Red Arcana (Offensive)
Focus on damage stats: physical attack, magic power, crit chance, attack speed.

### Blue Arcana (Utility)
Focus on utility stats: movement speed, lifesteal, cooldown reduction, mana regen.

### Green Arcana (Penetration/Defense)
Focus on penetration and defense: armor pen, magic pen, armor, magic resist, health.

---

## Universal Arcana Pages

Building arcana takes time and gold. You can't afford to build a unique page for every hero. Here are the two pages that cover 90% of heroes:

### Physical Damage Page (ADCs, Fighters, Assassins)

| Color | Arcana | Stat |
|-------|--------|------|
| Red (x10) | Cataclysm | Crit rate + crit damage |
| Blue (x10) | Hunt | Attack speed + movement speed |
| Green (x10) | Eagle Eye | Physical penetration |

This page works for: Hou Yi, Marco Polo, Li Bai, Han Xin, Wukong, Lu Bu, Mulan, Zilong, and most other physical damage heroes.

### Magic Damage Page (Mages)

| Color | Arcana | Stat |
|-------|--------|------|
| Red (x10) | Nightmare | Magic penetration + ability power |
| Blue (x10) | Hunt | Movement speed (same blue as physical page!) |
| Green (x10) | Mind's Eye | Magic penetration |

This page works for: Angela, Daji, Diaochan, Kongming, Nuwa, and most other mages.

---

## Build Order: What to Prioritize

If you're new, build your arcana in this order:

1. **Physical Damage Page first.** It covers the most heroes (ADC, fighter, assassin, some tanks).
2. **Magic Damage Page second.** It covers all your mages.
3. **Tank Page (optional).** If you main support or tank, a tank page (HP, armor, magic resist) is useful, but the physical page works fine for tanks too.

Don't spread your resources across multiple pages. Finish one page completely before starting the next.

---

## Common Arcana Mistakes

1. **Mixing arcana types.** Don't put magic pen on your physical page. Don't put crit on your mage page. Keep them focused.

2. **Upgrading arcana one at a time.** You want all 30 arcana at the same level before upgrading any of them. A full set of level 1 arcana is better than three level 5 arcana and 27 empty slots.

3. **Building niche pages too early.** A specialized page for one hero is a luxury. Build your universal pages first.

4. **Ignoring movement speed.** Hunt (blue arcana) gives movement speed, which is the most underrated stat in the game. Every role benefits from it.

---

## Quick Takeaways

- Build your Physical Damage page first (Cataclysm + Hunt + Eagle Eye)
- Build your Magic Damage page second (Nightmare + Hunt + Mind's Eye)
- Finish one full page before starting the next
- Movement speed is underrated — Hunt is the best blue arcana for most heroes
- A full arcana page gives you a free item's worth of stats at level 1
