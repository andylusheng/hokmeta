# How to Counter Nuwa: Hard Counters and Draft Strategies

*by Marcus Tran · Updated June 2026 · 6 min read*

---

Nuwa is a control mage who specializes in zone control and long-range poke. Her walls block movement, her lightning chains through enemies, and her ultimate is a global-range laser. She's not the most popular mid laner, but a good Nuwa can single-handedly control the map.

---

## 3 Best Counters to Nuwa

### 1. Prince of Lanling (Jungle)

Nuwa is immobile and squishy. Lanling goes invisible and kills her before she can react. This is one of the most one-sided matchups in the game.

**How to play it:** Camp mid lane. Every time Nuwa walks past river, kill her. She has no escape tools against an invisible assassin.

### 2. Han Xin (Jungle)

Han Xin's mobility lets him bypass Nuwa's walls and close the gap before she can reposition. His knockups prevent her from casting.

**How to play it:** Use Skill 1 to dash over her walls. Knock her up, kill her, leave.

### 3. Guan Yu (Clash Lane)

Guan Yu's speed lets him run past Nuwa's walls and his CC chain prevents her from casting anything.

**How to play it:** Charge through her walls with your passive, knock her up, and your team follows up.

---

## General Tips vs Nuwa

- Don't stand in corridors where her walls trap you.
- Her ultimate is global — watch the minimap when you're low.
- She's immobile. Any dive hero destroys her.
