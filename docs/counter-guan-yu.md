# How to Counter Guan Yu: The Heroes That Stop the Unstoppable

*by Marcus Tran · Updated June 2026 · 7 min read*

---

Guan Yu is the hero that feels like he's everywhere at once. He charges through your team, knocks everyone up, and rides away before you can target him. His map presence is unmatched among Clash Laners.

But his entire kit revolves around his charged state. If you can interrupt his charge or CC him before he engages, he's just a slow melee hero on a horse.

---

## 3 Best Counters to Guan Yu

### 1. Donghuang (Roaming/Support)

One last time. Donghuang's suppression stops Guan Yu's charge, prevents his knockup, and locks him in place. Without his charge, Guan Yu has no engage.

**How to play it:** Save your ult for Guan Yu. When he charges toward your team, suppress him before he reaches your carries.

### 2. Mulan (Clash Lane)

Mulan's silence from Light Sword form prevents Guan Yu from charging. No charge = no knockup = no damage.

**How to play it:** Engage in Light Sword, stack silence, swap to Heavy Sword, and kill him while he's silenced.

### 3. Angela (Mid Lane)

Angela's stun stops Guan Yu's charge cold. If he's stunned mid-charge, he's stuck in your team with no escape.

**How to play it:** When Guan Yu charges toward your team, throw Skill 2 directly in his path. The stun stops him, and your team collapses.

---

## General Tips vs Guan Yu

- **Interrupt his charge.** Any CC during his charge animation cancels it.
- **Ward the river.** Guan Yu roams through river. Vision prevents his flanks.
- **Don't clump up.** His knockup is AoE. Spread out.
- **He falls off late.** Guan Yu is strongest in the midgame. If you survive to late game, he's manageable.
