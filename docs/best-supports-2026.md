# Best Supports in Honor of Kings 2026: Which Healers and Enchanters Are Worth Your Time

*by Marcus Tran · Updated June 2026 · 7 min read*

---

Support is the most underrated role in Honor of Kings. A good support controls vision, peels for carries, and enables plays. A great support wins games without anyone noticing.

Here are the best supports in June 2026.

---

## S-Tier Supports

### Da Qiao — The Game Changer

Da Qiao is the best support in the game and arguably the best hero overall. Her ultimate teleports her entire team to her location. Her skills provide movement speed, healing, and CC. She enables strategies that no other hero can.

**Playstyle:** Roam constantly. Your job is map presence, not babysitting. Teleport your team to objectives, ganks, and teamfights.

**Weaknesses:** Banned in most high-elo games. If you get to play her, enjoy the free win.

### Yaria — The Untargetable Enchanter

Yaria attaches to an ally and becomes untargetable. She provides shields, healing, and CC while being completely safe. Attached to a fed carry, she makes them unkillable.

**Playstyle:** Attach to your best player. Keep them alive. Win.

**Weaknesses:** Useless if your team is behind. Relies on having at least one competent teammate.

---

## A-Tier Supports

### Dolia — The Playmaker

Dolia has strong CC, decent healing, and a kit that enables aggressive plays. She's the best support for initiating fights.

### Dyadia — The Protector

Dyadia specializes in keeping a single carry alive. Her shields and heals are massive, and her CC can peel divers off your backline.

### Liang — The Disruptor

Liang's CC chain is one of the longest in the game. If he catches someone, they're not moving for several seconds. Great for setting up kills and peeling.

---

## B-Tier: Solid and Reliable

Donghuang, Zhang Fei, Kui, Liu Shan, Ming, Mozi, Cai Yan, Lian Po, Zhuangzi — all viable, all have clear strengths, but none of them warp the game like Da Qiao or Yaria.

---

## Which Support Should You Play?

- **Want to carry?** Da Qiao (if she's not banned).
- **Playing with a duo?** Yaria. Attach to them and win.
- **Solo queue?** Dolia or Donghuang. Self-sufficient and always useful.
- **New to support?** Cai Yan. Simple healing support, hard to mess up.
