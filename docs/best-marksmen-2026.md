# Best Marksmen in Honor of Kings 2026: Who's Dominating Farm Lane

*by Marcus Tran · Updated June 2026 · 7 min read*

---

The Farm Lane in 2026 is in a balanced state. No S-tier marksmen are running the game, but the A-tier picks are all viable and have clear identities. Here's who you should be playing.

---

## A-Tier Marksmen

### Arli — The Untouchable

Arli has the highest skill ceiling of any marksman. Her passive lets her reposition after every ability, which means a good Arli is nearly impossible to catch. She's the best blind-pick marksman when you don't know what you're facing.

**Playstyle:** Kite-heavy. Stay at max range, reposition constantly, never let melee heroes touch you.

### Marco Polo — The Safest Pick

Marco Polo has a low-cooldown dash and true damage on his passive. He's safe in lane, scales well, and his ultimate can win teamfights by itself.

**Playstyle:** Poke with Skill 1, dash to reposition, ult when the enemy CC is on cooldown.

### Consort Yu — The Anti-Assassin

Consort Yu has a self-peel ultimate that knocks back and slows enemies. She's the best marksman when the enemy team has multiple divers.

**Playstyle:** Farm safely, use ult to self-peel, shred tanks with sustained damage.

### Hou Yi — The Beginner's Best Friend

Hou Yi is simple, effective, and his global ultimate can win games from across the map. He's the best marksman for new players and remains viable at every rank.

**Playstyle:** Auto-attack from range, use ult to initiate fights or peel for yourself.

### Lady Sun — The Lane Bully

Lady Sun wins almost every lane matchup. Her range and damage make her oppressive in the early game, and she snowballs hard if she gets a lead.

**Playstyle:** Aggressive laning. Poke the enemy out of lane, take tower, rotate.

---

## B-Tier: Situational Picks

Alessio, Di Renjie, Fang, Garo, Luban No.7, Shouyue — these marksmen work in specific comps or as counterpicks, but they're not blind-pickable.

---

## C-Tier: Outclassed

Huang Zhong and Meng Ya are the weakest marksmen right now. They have clear weaknesses that are easily exploited by competent opponents.

---

## Which Marksman Should You Play?

- **New to HOK?** Hou Yi. Simple, effective, teaches good habits.
- **Want to carry?** Arli or Marco Polo. High skill ceiling, high reward.
- **Getting dove every game?** Consort Yu. Her self-peel is unmatched.
- **Want to win lane?** Lady Sun. She bullies everyone.
