# Hou Yi Guide: Still the Best Beginner Marksman in 2026?

*by Marcus Tran · Updated June 2026 · 9 min read*

---

Hou Yi is the first marksman most HOK players learn. He's simple, straightforward, and effective. His damage is high, his ultimate is a global stun, and his AoE arrows can shred through multiple enemies at once.

But there's a gap between a "simple" Hou Yi and a "good" Hou Yi. Positioning matters more on him than on any other marksman because he has no dash. None. If you get caught, you die.

---

## Skills Breakdown

### Passive: Sun's Wrath

Hou Yi's basic attacks slow enemies. Attacks against slowed enemies deal bonus damage.

This is what makes him a kiting machine. You auto someone, they get slowed, you keep autoing, they can't escape. If your positioning is good, melee heroes should never touch you.

### Skill 1: Arrow Rain

Hou Yi fires a volley of arrows in a cone, dealing damage to all enemies hit.

This is your wave clear and your teamfight damage. The cone is wide and the damage is good. Use it to push waves and to hit multiple enemies in teamfights.

### Skill 2: Scorching Arrow

Hou Yi fires a powerful arrow in a line that deals damage and applies a stronger slow.

This is your poke and your setup. Hit someone with Skill 2, they're slowed, and then your passive kicks in for bonus damage on every subsequent auto.

### Skill 3 (Ultimate): Sun Arrow

Hou Yi fires a massive arrow across the map. It stuns the first enemy hero hit, with the stun duration increasing based on travel distance. Nearby enemies also take damage.

This is a global ultimate. You can fire it from base to top lane and stun someone for up to 3.5 seconds. In teamfights, you can use it at close range for a shorter but still impactful stun.

The cross-map snipes are satisfying, but don't hold your ult waiting for the perfect shot. Using it in a nearby fight is almost always better.

## Positioning Tips

Hou Yi's biggest weakness is his complete lack of mobility. No dash, no speed boost, no blink. Here's how to survive:

1. **Stay behind your frontline.** Always. If you can't see your tank on your screen, you're too far forward.
2. **Save your flash for emergencies.** Don't flash to chase kills. Flash to live.
3. **Ward your flanks.** If you don't know where the enemy assassin is, assume they're behind you.
4. **Use your ult to peel for yourself.** A point-blank Sun Arrow will stun a diving assassin long enough for your team to react.

---

## Quick Takeaways

- You slow on every auto. Use that to kite melee heroes.
- Your ult is global. Use it to help other lanes even when you can't rotate.
- No dash means positioning is everything. One mistake and you're dead.
- In teamfights, hit whoever is closest. Don't walk past their tank to hit their backline.
