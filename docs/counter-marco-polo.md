# How to Counter Marco Polo: What to Pick When the Enemy Locks Him

*by Marcus Tran · Updated June 2026 · 7 min read*

---

Marco Polo is the slippery marksman that never dies. He dashes out of every engage, pokes you from range, and if you ever clump up, his ultimate shreds your entire team.

But he has a weakness: his dash is directional and predictable, and his ultimate makes him stationary (he can move during it, but slowly). Catch him at the right moment and he's dead.

---

## 3 Best Counters to Marco Polo

### 1. Donghuang (Roaming/Support)

The pattern continues. Donghuang's suppression stops Marco Polo from dashing, which means he can't escape. Suppressed Marco Polo is a dead Marco Polo.

**How to play it:** Flash-ult Marco Polo when he's in range. He can't dash out of suppression.

### 2. Daji (Mid Lane)

Daji's stun is point-and-click. Marco Polo can't dash out of it. And Daji's full combo — stun into fireballs into ultimate — kills Marco Polo from full HP.

**How to play it:** Wait in a bush. When Marco Polo walks by, stun him and unload everything. He dies before the stun ends.

### 3. Prince of Lanling (Jungle)

Lanling goes invisible and bursts Marco Polo before he can react. Marco Polo's dash doesn't help if he doesn't see the threat coming.

**How to play it:** Go invisible, flank the backline, kill Marco Polo in under a second.

---

## General Tips vs Marco Polo

- **Don't clump up.** His ultimate deals AoE damage. Spread out.
- **CC him during his dash.** He's not immune during the dash.
- **Build armor.** Marco Polo deals physical damage.
- **Don't chase him.** He's faster than you.
