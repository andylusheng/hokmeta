# How to Counter Han Xin: Stop the Snowball Before It Starts

*by Marcus Tran · Updated June 2026 · 7 min read*

---

A fed Han Xin is one of the scariest things in Honor of Kings. He clears your jungle, pushes your towers, and is gone before you can even rotate. He doesn't teamfight — he just wins through map pressure.

But Han Xin has a glaring weakness: his early game is fragile, and if you shut him down before he gets items, he never becomes a threat.

---

## Why Han Xin Snowballs

Han Xin's kit is built for farming. Fast clear speed, high mobility, and the ability to split push means he can accumulate a gold lead without ever fighting. Once he's ahead on items, he can 1v1 almost anyone and escape any gank.

The key is stopping him before he gets there.

---

## 3 Best Counters to Han Xin

### 1. Fatih (Jungle)

Fatih is currently the best jungler in the game, and he matches Han Xin in every category: clear speed, mobility, and dueling power. More importantly, Fatih can invade Han Xin's jungle and kill him at level 2-3 before Han Xin's mobility comes online.

**How to play it:** Start Red Buff, immediately invade Han Xin's Blue side. Han Xin's early clear leaves him low on HP. Catch him at his Blue and it's a free kill. Repeat every time your invade is up.

### 2. Musashi (Jungle)

Musashi is another S-tier jungler who outpaces Han Xin. His clear is faster, his burst is higher, and his CC can lock Han Xin down before he dashes away.

**How to play it:** Same as Fatih — invade early and often. Musashi's level 3 is stronger than Han Xin's. Force fights at his buffs.

### 3. Donghuang (Roaming/Support)

At this point you're seeing a pattern. Donghuang's suppression is the answer to every mobile hero in the game. Han Xin can't dash out of a suppression, and without his mobility, he's dead in under a second.

**How to play it:** When Han Xin dives your backline or tries to steal an objective, ult him. Your team collapses and he dies.

---

## General Tips vs Han Xin

- **Ward his jungle.** Knowing where Han Xin is at all times is half the battle. If you can track him, you can avoid his ganks and counter his invades.
- **Invade him early.** Han Xin's level 1-3 is his weakest point. If you let him free-farm to level 4, you've already lost.
- **Defend your towers.** Han Xin wins by split pushing. If you let him take tier 1 towers for free, he snowballs out of control.
- **Don't chase him.** Han Xin has more mobility than you. Chasing him means wasting time while he takes objectives elsewhere.

---

## What NOT to Do

- Don't ignore him and hope he goes away. Han Xin doesn't go away. He gets stronger.
- Don't pick slow junglers into him (Dian Wei, Butterfly). He'll steal all your camps.
- Don't leave side lanes undefended. Han Xin will punish every undefended tower.
