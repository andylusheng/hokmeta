# Best Tanks in Honor of Kings 2026: Front Line Heroes That Actually Win Games

*by Marcus Tran · Updated June 2026 · 6 min read*

---

Tanks in Honor of Kings have one job: absorb damage, apply CC, and create space for your carries. A good tank makes the enemy team waste their cooldowns on you while your damage dealers clean up.

---

## A-Tier Tanks

### Dun — The All-Rounder

Dun is the best tank in the game right now. He has damage, CC, sustain, and a game-changing ultimate that can engage or disengage. He works in Clash Lane and as a roaming support.

**Playstyle:** Frontline engage. Hook enemies with your ultimate, CC them, and let your team follow up.

### Zhang Fei — The Wall

Zhang Fei is the definition of a tank. His ultimate transforms him into a massive beast with bonus HP and AoE knockback. He's the best tank for protecting your backline.

**Playstyle:** Peel for your carries. Stand between them and the enemy team. Ult to push divers away.

### Lian Po — The Unkillable

Lian Po's passive gives him damage reduction while casting skills, which makes him one of the tankiest heroes in the game during teamfights. He's hard to kill and his CC is consistent.

**Playstyle:** Initiate fights. Walk into the enemy team, CC everyone, and trust your damage dealers to follow up.

---

## B-Tier Tanks

Kui, Liu Shan, Donghuang, Xiang Yu, Arthur — these tanks are viable but more situational than the A-tier picks.

---

## Which Tank Should You Play?

- **Want to engage?** Dun. His hook ultimate is one of the best engage tools in the game.
- **Need to protect your ADC?** Zhang Fei. Nobody peels better.
- **Want to never die?** Lian Po. His damage reduction makes him absurdly tanky.
