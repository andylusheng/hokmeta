# Nezha Guide: The Mobile Menace You're Sleeping On

*by Marcus Tran · Updated June 2026 · 9 min read*

---

Nezha is one of those heroes that nobody talks about — until he's 8/0 in your ranked game and you're wondering why he's not in every tier list.

His kit is deceptively simple but his global ultimate makes him one of the best roamers in Honor of Kings. He can be in the Clash Lane farming one second, and diving the enemy marksman across the map the next. If you like making the enemy team paranoid, Nezha is your hero.

---

## Skills Breakdown

### Passive: Blazing Trail

Nezha's skills leave burning zones on the ground. Enemies standing in these zones take damage over time.

This is free damage in lane and in teamfights. Drop your skills on the wave, on the enemy, on objectives — the burn adds up.

### Skill 1: Flame Sweep

Nezha spins and deals damage in a circle. Enemies hit take burn damage and are slowed.

This is your wave clear and your main damage tool in lane. The slow helps you stick to enemies for follow-up autos.

### Skill 2: Fire Wheel

Nezha dashes forward on his wind-fire wheels, dealing damage to enemies in his path. He gains movement speed after the dash.

This is your mobility skill. Short cooldown, decent damage, speed boost afterward. Use it to gap-close, escape, and roam between lanes.

### Skill 3 (Ultimate): Heaven's Wrath

Nezha locks onto an enemy hero anywhere on the map and flies to them, dealing massive AoE damage on landing and knocking them back.

This is what makes Nezha special. Global-range ultimate. You see the enemy marksman farming alone in bot lane? You ult them from top lane. You see a low-HP enemy running to base? You ult them. Teamfight breaking out at Dragon? You ult the enemy jungler to steal the objective.

The knockback on landing can also push enemies into your team or away from objectives.

---

## The Combo

Basic engage:

**Skill 2 (close distance) → Skill 1 (spin + slow) → auto-attack until they try to run → Ultimate (chase them down)**

Or for cross-map plays:

**Ultimate (fly to target) → Skill 1 on landing → Skill 2 to chase → auto-attack to finish**

## When to Pick Nezha

Nezha excels when:
- The enemy team has squishy carries you can ult and delete
- Your team needs a roamer who can join fights from anywhere
- The enemy comp lacks CC to stop your ult

He struggles against:
- Heavy CC (Donghuang, Zhang Fei) that stops your dive
- Tanks that can body-block your ult
- Fast junglers who can punish your roams by taking your towers

---

## Quick Takeaways

- Your ult is global. Check the minimap constantly for picks.
- Use Skill 2 to roam between lanes — you're faster than most heroes.
- The burn from your passive adds up. Don't ignore it.
- You're not a tank. Build bruiser, not full tank.
