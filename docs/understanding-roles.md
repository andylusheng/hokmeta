# Understanding Roles in Honor of Kings: What Does Each Position Do?

*by Marcus Tran · Updated June 2026 · 9 min read*

---

If you're coming from another MOBA, Honor of Kings roles will look familiar but with some key differences. If you're new to MOBAs entirely, this guide will explain what each position does, what heroes go there, and how to tell if you're doing your job right.

---

## The Five Positions

### Clash Lane (Top Lane)

**What you do:** You're the solo laner. Your job is to win (or at least not lose) your 1v1 matchup, pressure the enemy tower, and join team fights in the mid to late game.

**Typical heroes:** Fighters (Lu Bu, Mulan, Guan Yu), tanks (Dun, Lian Po), and some assassins (Nezha)

**You're doing well if:**
- You're even or ahead in gold vs. your lane opponent
- You've taken the first tower or are pressuring it
- You rotate to Dragon fights when they happen
- You're not dying to ganks

**You're struggling if:**
- You're 0/3 and two levels behind
- The enemy Clash Laner is roaming and killing your team while you're stuck farming
- You're losing tower for free

---

### Mid Lane

**What you do:** You control the center of the map. Your job is to clear waves, roam to side lanes, and deal magic damage in team fights.

**Typical heroes:** Mages (Angela, Daji, Diaochan, Kongming, Nuwa)

**You're doing well if:**
- You're clearing waves and rotating to help other lanes
- You're not dying to ganks (mid is the easiest lane to gank)
- You're dealing consistent damage in team fights
- You're securing kills with your burst damage

**You're struggling if:**
- You're constantly pushed under tower
- You're getting ganked and dying repeatedly
- You're roaming but not getting anything done

---

### Farm Lane (ADC / Marksman)

**What you do:** You're the team's primary source of sustained physical damage. Your job is to farm gold, scale into the late game, and carry team fights.

**Typical heroes:** Marksmen (Hou Yi, Marco Polo, Arli, Consort Yu, Lady Sun)

**You're doing well if:**
- You have the most gold on your team (or close to it)
- You're not dying in team fights
- You're dealing consistent damage to objectives and enemies
- You're positioning safely and kiting effectively

**You're struggling if:**
- You're behind on gold
- You're dying first in every team fight
- You're pushing without vision and getting ganked

---

### Jungle

**What you do:** You're the playmaker. Your job is to farm jungle camps, gank lanes, secure objectives (Dragon, Lord), and control the map.

**Typical heroes:** Assassins (Fatih, Lam, Li Bai, Han Xin, Prince of Lanling), fighters (Musashi, Sun Ce)

**You're doing well if:**
- You're securing Dragons and Lords
- Your ganks are resulting in kills or tower pressure
- You're tracking the enemy jungler and countering their plays
- You're ahead in gold and levels

**You're struggling if:**
- The enemy jungler is invading you and stealing your camps
- You're losing every Dragon and Lord
- Your ganks are failing and you're falling behind

---

### Roaming (Support)

**What you do:** You enable your team. Your job is to provide vision, peel for carries, and set up plays.

**Typical heroes:** Tanks (Zhang Fei, Donghuang, Dun, Lian Po), enchanters (Da Qiao, Yaria, Cai Yan)

**You're doing well if:**
- Your carries are alive and dealing damage
- You have vision control around objectives
- Your CC is landing on priority targets
- You're roaming effectively and impacting multiple lanes

**You're struggling if:**
- Your ADC is dying repeatedly
- You're getting caught while warding
- You're not sure where to be on the map

---

## How to Choose Your Role

If you're new, try each role for a few games and see what clicks:

- **Like 1v1 duels?** Play Clash Lane.
- **Like burst damage and roaming?** Play Mid Lane.
- **Like scaling and carrying late?** Play Farm Lane.
- **Like controlling the map and making plays?** Play Jungle.
- **Like enabling others and being the unsung hero?** Play Support.

Most players main one role and have a backup role they can play competently. Pick one to focus on, learn it deeply, and branch out later.

---

## Quick Takeaways

- Five roles: Clash, Mid, Farm, Jungle, Roaming
- Each role has a distinct job and typical hero pool
- Learn one role well before branching out
- The best role is the one you enjoy most
