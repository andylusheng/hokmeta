# Zilong Guide: The Warrior That Refuses to Fall Off

*by Marcus Tran · Updated June 2026 · 9 min read*

---

Zilong (Zhao Yun) is one of the original Honor of Kings heroes. He's been in the game since launch, and unlike some of the other old-guard fighters, he's never really been bad. He's not always meta-defining, but he's always playable.

Why? Because his kit is fundamentally sound. Dash, knockup, damage reduction, healing, and an ultimate that makes him nearly unkillable for a few seconds. He's a diver, a duelist, and a surprisingly effective jungler.

---

## Skills Breakdown

### Passive: Dragon's Might

Zilong takes reduced damage when his HP is low. The lower his HP, the more damage reduction he gets.

This is what makes Zilong win fights that look unwinnable. You get low, the enemy thinks they can finish you, but your passive kicks in and suddenly you're tanking hits like a full-tank while still dealing fighter damage.

### Skill 1: Thunder Strike

Zilong dashes forward and his next basic attack deals bonus damage and slows the target.

This is your gap closer and your chase tool. The enhanced auto after the dash hits hard, especially with items.

### Skill 2: Cloud Piercer

Zilong thrusts his spear forward multiple times, dealing damage and healing for each hit.

This is your main damage and sustain ability. It hits in a line, so position yourself to hit as many enemies as possible. The healing scales with your attack damage, which is why you build damage on Zilong, not tank.

### Skill 3 (Ultimate): Battle Cry

Zilong roars and gains a massive attack damage boost for a short duration. During the ultimate, his basic attacks deal bonus damage.

This is your "I win this fight" button. Pop it before a duel, during a teamfight, or when diving the backline. The damage boost is significant and the duration is long enough to get multiple kills if you're positioned well.

---

## The Combo

Basic engage:

**Skill 1 (dash in) → enhanced auto → Skill 2 (thrust + heal) → auto-attack → Ultimate (when they try to fight back) → auto-attack until they're dead**

For diving backline:

**Ultimate first → Skill 1 to close distance → Skill 2 to burst → auto-attack to finish**

## When to Pick Zilong

Zilong works when:
- Your team needs a diver who can threaten the backline
- You're playing Clash Lane against a melee matchup
- You're jungling and need a fighter-assassin hybrid

He struggles against:
- Ranged Clash laners who can poke him down
- Heavy CC that prevents him from using his passive effectively
- Burst mages who can one-shot him before his damage reduction kicks in

---

## Quick Takeaways

- Your passive makes you tankier at low HP. Don't run — fight when you're low.
- Skill 2 heals you. Use it aggressively in fights.
- Your ultimate is a massive damage steroid. Don't waste it on minions.
- You can jungle or Clash Lane. Both work.
