# How to Counter Wukong: The Real Answer (And What Actually Works)

*by Marcus Tran · Updated June 2026 · 7 min read*

---

"Just CC him" is what everyone says about countering Wukong. And yeah, that works. But there's more to it than that. Wukong players who know what they're doing will wait for your CC to be on cooldown before engaging. So you need a plan B.

---

## Why Wukong Is Scary

Wukong's damage comes from crits. His passive gives him free crit chance from level 1, and his Skill 1 guarantees a crit on his next basic attack. When he's ahead, two autos kill a squishy. When he's even, three autos. When he's behind, he still does enough damage to be a threat.

But Wukong is melee. He has to walk up to you. And his only defensive tool (Skill 1 shield) blocks exactly one ability.

---

## 3 Best Counters to Wukong

### 1. Donghuang (Roaming/Support)

Surprise — Donghuang counters another mobile assassin. His suppression ignores Wukong's Skill 1 shield and prevents him from auto-attacking. Suppressed Wukong = dead Wukong.

**How to play it:** Stand on your carry. When Wukong goes invisible (his Skill 1 or bush play), ult him the moment he appears.

### 2. Zhang Fei (Roaming/Support)

Zhang Fei's ultimate knocks Wukong away from your carries. His shield absorbs Wukong's initial burst. And his tankiness means Wukong can't kill him even with a full combo.

**How to play it:** Position between Wukong and your carries. Ult him away when he engages.

### 3. Mulan (Clash Lane)

Mulan's silence prevents Wukong from using his skills, which means he can't get his enhanced crit autos. No enhanced crits = no damage.

**How to play it:** Engage Wukong with Light Sword form, stack silence, swap to Heavy Sword, kill him.

---

## Item Counters

- **Cuirass of Savagery:** Armor reduces Wukong's crit damage significantly.
- **Blazing Cape:** The burn damage helps reveal Wukong when he's trying to reposition.

---

## General Tips vs Wukong

- **Don't face-check bushes.** Wukong players love camping bushes.
- **Ward your flanks.** Wukong wants to come from the side or behind.
- **Stick together.** Wukong wants isolated targets. If you're grouped, he can't pick anyone off.
- **Build armor early.** One armor item on a squishy can be the difference between living and dying.

---

## What NOT to Do

- Don't pick squishy, immobile heroes into him without a plan.
- Don't chase him into unwarded jungle.
- Don't ignore his farming. An underfarmed Wukong is a non-threat.
