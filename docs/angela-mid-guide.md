# Angela Guide: The Mid Laner That Makes Everyone Rage

*by Marcus Tran · Updated June 2026 · 9 min read*

---

Angela is the hero you pick when you just want to watch things die. She's not complicated. She's not subtle. She's a burst mage with a point-and-click stun, massive AoE damage, and a playstyle that basically amounts to "see enemy, delete enemy."

She's also one of the most popular mid laners in Honor of Kings, especially at lower ranks, because she punishes bad positioning harder than almost anyone. Walk too close to a bush? Stunned. Stand still for half a second? Ulted. Game over.

---

## Skills Breakdown

### Passive: Fire Mastery

Angela's skills apply burn stacks. At a certain number of stacks, the target takes bonus damage.

This is why her full combo deletes people even when you're slightly behind. The burn stacks amplify everything.

### Skill 1: Flame Surge

Angela launches fireballs in a line, dealing damage to all enemies hit.

This is your poke, your wave clear, and your main damage ability. The range is good and the cooldown is short. Spam it.

### Skill 2: Chaos Vortex

Angela throws a fireball that stuns the first enemy hit and creates a burning zone that damages enemies over time.

This is the setup. Land this stun and your entire combo is guaranteed. Miss this stun and you're standing there looking stupid while the enemy jungler kills you.

The burning zone also slows, which means even if the stun is short, enemies can't easily walk out of your follow-up damage.

### Skill 3 (Ultimate): Blazing Inferno

Angela channels a massive beam of fire in a target direction, dealing continuous damage to all enemies in the beam. She can rotate the beam during the channel. She also gains a shield while channeling.

This is one of the most satisfying ultimates in the game. The damage is absurd if you hit the full channel. The beam is wide enough to hit multiple enemies in a teamfight. And the shield means you're harder to kill while casting.

The downside: you can't move during the channel. If you ult at the wrong time, you're a stationary target.

---

## The Combo

Standard kill combo:

**Skill 2 (stun) → Skill 1 (fireballs) → Ultimate (beam)**

If you land the stun, everything else is guaranteed. Against squishy targets, this kills from full HP at level 4. Against tankier targets, you might need to add another Skill 1 rotation.

## When to Pick Angela

Angela is great when:
- Enemy team has immobile carries you can stun
- Your team needs burst damage to delete a specific threat
- You're playing against melee-heavy comps that have to walk through your Skill 2 zone

She's terrible when:
- Enemy has multiple dashes that can dodge your stun
- Enemy has long-range poke that outranges you
- Enemy jungler is camping your lane and you have no vision

---

## Quick Takeaways

- Everything starts with Skill 2. If you miss the stun, back off.
- Use bushes. Angela is one of the best bush-campers in the game.
- Your ult gives a shield. Don't cancel it early if you're taking damage.
- In teamfights, stay behind your frontline and look for picks.
