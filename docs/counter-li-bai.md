# How to Counter Li Bai: 3 Heroes That Make Him Useless

*by Marcus Tran · Updated June 2026 · 7 min read*

---

Li Bai is one of the most frustrating heroes to play against. He dashes in, deletes your carry, and teleports back to safety before you can even target him. It feels like there's no counterplay.

But there is. Li Bai's entire kit relies on specific timing windows and positioning. Disrupt either one and he's a sitting duck.

---

## Why Li Bai Feels Broken

Li Bai's combo — dash in with Skill 1, drop Skill 2 for the I-frame, ult, then dash back — makes him functionally invulnerable during his damage window. He's untargetable during Skill 2 and his ultimate, and he's back at his starting position before you can retaliate.

The weaknesses: he needs 4 passive stacks to ult, his Skill 1 leaves a visible afterimage, and he's extremely squishy if you catch him.

---

## 3 Best Counters to Li Bai

### 1. Donghuang (Roaming/Support)

Donghuang is Li Bai's hardest counter, period. His ultimate suppresses Li Bai through his I-frames. Li Bai can't become untargetable if he's suppressed. He can't dash back to his afterimage. He just stands there and dies.

**How to play it:** Save your ult exclusively for Li Bai. When you see him dash in, wait for him to commit (Skill 2 or ult), then suppress him. Your team will kill him before the suppression ends. If Donghuang is on the enemy team, Li Bai is effectively unplayable.

### 2. Zhang Fei (Roaming/Support)

Zhang Fei's ultimate is a massive AoE knockback that disrupts Li Bai's entire engage. If Li Bai dashes in and gets knocked back before he can use Skill 2 or ult, he's stuck in your team with no escape.

**How to play it:** Position yourself between Li Bai's afterimage and your carries. When he dashes in, ult him away from your team. He either wastes his combo on nothing or has to dash back with no value.

### 3. Fatih (Jungle)

Fatih counters Li Bai by being faster and deadlier. He clears jungle camps faster, which means he invades Li Bai's jungle and starves him of resources. An underfarmed Li Bai can't one-shot anyone.

**How to play it:** Invade Li Bai's jungle at level 2-3. Steal his camps. Track his position and counter-gank his lanes. If Li Bai is behind on items, his combo tickles instead of killing.

---

## General Tips vs Li Bai

- **Watch his afterimage.** Li Bai's Skill 1 leaves a visible mark where he started. If you see it, you know where he's going to return. Camp it with CC.
- **CC him during his Skill 1 dashes.** He's not untargetable during the dash. A well-timed stun stops his engage cold.
- **Don't clump up.** Li Bai's ultimate deals reduced damage to multiple targets. Spread out and his ult loses value.
- **Build armor.** Li Bai deals physical damage. One armor item on your carry can be the difference between getting one-shot and surviving with 20% HP.

---

## What NOT to Do

- Don't chase Li Bai. He's faster than you and has two dashes plus a teleport.
- Don't pick squishy, immobile heroes into him. Daji, Xiao Qiao, and Luban No.7 are free kills for Li Bai.
- Don't ignore his farming. Li Bai needs items. Invade him, deny his camps, and he's useless.
