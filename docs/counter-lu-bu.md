# How to Counter Lu Bu: 5 Heroes That Shut Him Down

*by Marcus Tran · Updated June 2026 · 7 min read*

---

Lu Bu is one of those heroes that feels impossible to deal with when he's ahead. He heals through your damage, his true damage ignores your armor, and his ultimate turns teamfights into a blender. But he's not unbeatable. Here's how to make him useless.

---

## Why Lu Bu Is Annoying

Lu Bu's whole kit is built around two things: healing through extended fights, and dealing true damage that ignores your defenses. If you try to stat-check him — just walk up and trade autos — you lose. Every time.

The counterplay is simple: **don't give him extended fights, and don't let him hit you.**

---

## 5 Best Counters to Lu Bu

### 1. Guan Yu (Clash Lane)

Guan Yu is Lu Bu's worst matchup. He's faster, he kites, and his CC interrupts Lu Bu's skills. Lu Bu can't heal if he can't hit you, and Guan Yu's hit-and-run playstyle means Lu Bu is always chasing but never catching.

**How to play it:** Charge in with Guan Yu's passive, knock Lu Bu up, deal damage, and run away before he can trade back. Repeat until he's low enough to all-in. Save your CC for his ultimate — if you knock him up during the cast, his ult goes on cooldown and he's stuck.

### 2. Mulan (Clash Lane)

Mulan's silence from her Light Sword passive shuts down Lu Bu's ability to chain skills together. If he can't use his skills, he can't proc his passive healing, and he's just a slow melee hero with no sustain.

**How to play it:** Engage in Light Sword form, stack the silence quickly, swap to Heavy Sword, and burst him down before the silence wears off. Don't let him drag the fight out.

### 3. Angela (Mid Lane)

Angela doesn't care about Lu Bu's healing because she kills him before it matters. A full combo from Angela — stun into fireballs into ultimate — deletes Lu Bu from full HP if she's even slightly ahead.

**How to play it:** Stay behind your frontline in teamfights. When Lu Bu jumps in with his ult, stun him immediately and unload everything. He can't heal through burst damage.

### 4. Donghuang (Roaming/Support)

Donghuang's ultimate suppresses a single target, and the suppressed target shares any damage Donghuang takes. If Lu Bu dives your backline, Donghuang ults him, and Lu Bu dies to his own team's damage.

**How to play it:** Save your ult for Lu Bu's engage. The moment he ults into your team, suppress him. Your team will kill him before the suppression ends.

### 5. Marco Polo (Farm Lane)

Marco Polo's true damage passive and his mobility make him a nightmare for Lu Bu. He can dash away from Lu Bu's engage and poke him down from range. If Lu Bu commits with his ult, Marco Polo can dash out and continue kiting.

**How to play it:** Stay at max range. Poke with Skill 1. When Lu Bu ults, dash out immediately. Never stand still.

---

## General Tips vs Lu Bu

- **Don't fight him in his ultimate zone.** The defensive buffs and healing make him nearly unkillable. Wait it out, then re-engage.
- **Build Grievous Wounds.** Anti-heal items reduce his sustain significantly.
- **Kite him.** Lu Bu has no dashes. If you're ranged, you should never be in melee range.
- **Don't stack HP.** His true damage ignores armor, so stacking health against him is better than stacking armor.

---

## What NOT to Do

- Don't pick melee fighters without mobility into him (Kaizer, Arthur, Biron). He out-sustains all of them.
- Don't fight him in the river or jungle where he can ult across walls.
- Don't ignore him in teamfights. A free-firing Lu Bu wins games.
