## Why Daji Gets MVPs

MVP in Honor of Kings isn't just about kills. It factors in damage dealt, kill participation, deaths, and objectives. Daji excels at all of them:

- **Damage output is absurd.** Her full combo on a squishy target deals more burst than almost any other mage in the game.
- **Kill participation is easy.** Her stun is point-and-click. You don't need to land a skillshot. You just need to be in range.
- **Deaths stay low.** You play behind your team, you stun whoever dives, and you leave. No risky dives required.
- **She scales into a one-shot machine.** Once you have 3+ items, every combo is a kill threat.

## Skills Breakdown

### Passive: Captivate

Every skill hit reduces the enemy's Magical Defense, stacking up to 3 times.

This is why your combo deletes people. By the time your ultimate lands, the target has already lost a chunk of magic defense. Your damage snowballs within a single rotation.

### Skill 1: Soul Impact

Daji fires a shockwave in a target direction, dealing magic damage to enemies hit.

This is your poke, your wave clear, and the first hit in your combo. Five-second cooldown means you can spam it constantly. Use it to push waves fast so you can roam.

### Skill 2: Strike a Pose

Daji casts a love spell at a target enemy, dealing damage and stunning them.

This is the MVP skill. Point-and-click stun. No skillshot. No prediction. You click a target, they get stunned, they die. The cooldown is 10 seconds, which is short enough that you'll have it for almost every engagement.

### Skill 3 (Ultimate): Heartbreaker

Daji unleashes 5 waves of damage that randomly distribute among enemies in range.

Here's the key: if there's only one enemy in range, all 5 hits land on them. That's why Daji is an assassination monster. Isolate a target, pop your ult, and watch all five hits delete them.

In teamfights with multiple enemies, the damage splits. It's still good AoE, but for MVP purposes, you want to use this on isolated or low-HP targets.

---

## The MVP Combo

Standard assassination combo:

**Skill 2 (stun) → Skill 1 (shockwave) → Ultimate (all 5 hits)**

Against squishy targets (marksmen, mages, assassins), this kills from full HP once you have 2-3 items. Against tanks, it takes a chunk but won't one-shot them.

Advanced tip: Use Flash + Skill 2 to catch enemies who think they're safe at range. Flash-stun into full combo is how you get those extra kills that push you into MVP territory.

## MVP Gameplay Strategy

### Early Game (Levels 1-4)

Push your wave with Skill 1 and look for roams. Daji at level 2 with Flash and Skill 2 is already a kill threat. Walk into side lane, stun the enemy, let your teammate finish them. First blood sets the tone.

Don't sit mid farming minions for 5 minutes. That's how you end up 0/0/2 with no MVP potential.

### Mid Game (Levels 5-12)

This is where Daji becomes terrifying. You have your ultimate, 2-3 items, and every squishy on the enemy team is a walking kill. Group with your jungler, find picks, and chain kills.

Your goal: be present at every kill. Stun first, combo second, collect the kill or assist. Kill participation is the fastest path to MVP.

### Late Game (Teamfights)

Stay behind your frontline. Wait for the enemy to commit. When their carry is in range, Flash + Skill 2 + full combo. Delete them before the fight even starts.

If you can't reach the carry, stun whoever dives your team. Peeling with your stun still gets you assists and keeps your deaths at zero.

---

## Common Mistakes That Cost You MVP

- **Diving alone into 3 enemies.** You're a burst mage, not an assassin with escapes. Get caught, you die, and your KDA tanks.
- **Using ultimate in a 5-man teamfight.** The damage splits 5 ways. Save it for isolated targets or cleanup.
- **Holding Skill 2 for "the perfect moment."** Stun someone. Anyone. A 1.5-second stun on a tank still sets up your team for a kill.
- **Ignoring waves to roam constantly.** If you lose your tower because you roamed too much, that's -gold and -map pressure. Push first, then roam.

## Quick Takeaways

- Skill 2 is point-and-click. There's no excuse to miss it.
- Your ultimate one-shots isolated squishies. Hunt them.
- Flash + Skill 2 is your engage. Use it aggressively.
- Stay alive. MVP requires a low death count more than a high kill count.
- Push waves fast, roam often, be at every kill.
