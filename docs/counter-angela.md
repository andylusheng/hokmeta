# How to Counter Angela: Itemization and Hero Picks That Destroy Her

*by Marcus Tran · Updated June 2026 · 7 min read*

---

Angela is the definition of a one-dimensional hero. She stuns you, she ults you, you die. If she misses the stun, she's a walking sack of gold. The entire counterplay to Angela revolves around one thing: don't get stunned.

---

## Why Angela Feels Unfair

Angela's full combo — Skill 2 (stun) → Skill 1 (fireballs) → Ultimate (beam) — can kill almost any squishy from full HP, even when she's behind. The stun is point-and-click (sort of — it's a projectile, but it's fast), and once you're stunned, you can't dodge the follow-up.

But her stun is a projectile. It can be dodged. It can be blocked. And if she misses it, she has nothing.

---

## 4 Best Counters to Angela

### 1. Prince of Lanling (Jungle)

Lanling is an assassin who can go invisible. Angela can't stun what she can't see. He appears behind her, bursts her in under a second, and she's dead before she can even cast.

**How to play it:** Go invisible, flank her in teamfights, and wait for her to use her stun on someone else. Then kill her. Even if she has her stun up, your burst is fast enough that she dies before the stun animation finishes.

### 2. Arke (Jungle)

Same concept as Lanling but from a different angle. Arke's passive gives her bonus damage when attacking from behind, and her ultimate lets her go invisible and reposition. Angela can't handle assassins who come from unexpected angles.

**How to play it:** Wait for Angela to use her stun. Go invisible, get behind her, and delete her with your passive-enhanced crits.

### 3. Guan Yu (Clash Lane)

Guan Yu is fast enough to dodge Angela's stun, and his CC chain can lock her down before she gets her combo off. Once she's knocked up and slowed, she's dead.

**How to play it:** Charge in from the side. Dodge her stun with your speed, knock her up, and kill her before she can recover.

### 4. Fatih (Jungle)

Fatih's dash lets him dodge Angela's stun on reaction. His burst damage kills her in one rotation. And his clear speed means he's always ahead on items.

**How to play it:** Bait her stun with a dash fake-out. Once it's on cooldown, engage and kill her.

## Item Counters

- **Crimson Shadow - Radiance:** The magic damage shield absorbs Angela's burst, giving you time to kill her or escape.
- **Blood Rage:** Magic resist plus a shield when you're low. Good on fighters and tanks.
- **Boots of Resistance:** CC reduction means her stun duration is shorter, giving you a window to dodge the follow-up.

---

## General Tips vs Angela

- **Bait her stun.** Most Angela players throw Skill 2 the moment they see you. Fake an engage, dodge the stun, then actually engage.
- **Don't stand in her burning zone.** Skill 2 leaves a zone that deals damage over time. If you're standing in it, you're taking free damage.
- **Flank her in teamfights.** Angela wants to stand behind her frontline and free-fire. If you come from the side or behind, she panics and misses her stun.
- **Build magic resist.** One MR item cuts her damage by 30-40%. It's worth it.

---

## What NOT to Do

- Don't walk in a straight line toward her. You're asking to get stunned.
- Don't clump up. Her ult hits multiple targets.
- Don't fight her in narrow jungle corridors where her stun is easier to land.
