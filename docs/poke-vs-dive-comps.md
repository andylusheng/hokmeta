# Poke Comp vs Dive Comp: Which Team Strategy Wins in the Current Meta?

*by Marcus Tran · Updated June 2026 · 9 min read*

---

Honor of Kings team comps generally fall into three categories: poke, dive, and protect. Each has strengths, weaknesses, and specific heroes that make them work. Understanding these archetypes will help you draft better and play to your comp's win condition.

---

## Poke Comps

### What They Do

Poke comps win by chipping the enemy down before fights. They avoid direct engages and instead wear the enemy out from range. When the enemy is low enough, they either retreat (giving up objectives) or fight at a disadvantage (and lose).

### Key Heroes

- **Mid:** Milady, Nuwa, Lady Zhen, Xiao Qiao, Zhou Yu
- **Farm Lane:** Marco Polo, Lady Sun, Shouyue
- **Jungle:** Musashi (can poke and engage)
- **Support:** Da Qiao, Liang

### How to Win with Poke

1. Control vision so you can see the enemy before they engage on you
2. Chip the enemy down before every objective fight
3. Don't force fights — let the enemy come to you
4. If the enemy hard-engages, disengage immediately and reset
5. Siege towers with range advantage

### How to Beat Poke

- Hard engage before they can chip you down
- Flank from multiple angles
- Pick dive heroes who can bypass their range

---

## Dive Comps

### What They Do

Dive comps win by jumping on the enemy backline and deleting their carries. They don't care about poke — they care about getting on top of the enemy ADC and mage as fast as possible.

### Key Heroes

- **Jungle:** Fatih, Lam, Prince of Lanling, Sun Ce
- **Clash Lane:** Lu Bu, Mulan, Guan Yu, Nezha
- **Support:** Donghuang, Dun, Lian Po
- **Mid:** Daji (point-and-click burst), Diaochan (reset machine)

### How to Win with Dive

1. Wait for the enemy to use their CC or peel abilities
2. Engage from multiple angles simultaneously
3. Focus the enemy ADC and mage — ignore the tank
4. End the fight fast — dive comps lose extended fights
5. Ward enemy flanks so you don't get counter-engaged

### How to Beat Dive

- Peel for your carries (Zhang Fei, Yaria, Donghuang)
- Spread out so they can't dive everyone at once
- Counter-engage on their backline when they dive yours

---

## Protect Comps

### What They Do

Protect comps revolve around a single hypercarry. The entire team's job is to keep that carry alive long enough to win the fight. This is the "funnel" strategy — pour all your resources into one player.

### Key Heroes

- **Hypercarry:** Arli, Consort Yu, Hou Yi
- **Support:** Yaria, Zhang Fei, Cai Yan, Ming
- **Tank:** Lian Po, Dun, Kui
- **Jungle:** Any jungler who can peel or provide utility

### How to Win with Protect

1. Your ADC is the win condition — keep them alive at all costs
2. Don't take fights when your ADC is dead or not present
3. Position so the ADC can free-fire without being threatened
4. Use CC to peel divers off your ADC
5. Play slow and scale — protect comps are strongest late game

### How to Beat Protect

- Kill the support first (especially Yaria)
- Dive the ADC from multiple angles
- Split push — protect comps struggle to respond to map pressure

---

## Which Comp Is Best in June 2026?

Right now, **dive comps are the strongest** in the current meta. The S-tier junglers (Fatih, Lam, Musashi, Sun Ce) are all divers, and they define how the game is played.

Poke comps are viable but require more coordination than most solo queue teams have. Protect comps work if you have a duo partner playing ADC + support.

**For solo queue:** Draft a balanced comp with at least one diver and one source of peel. Flexibility beats rigid archetypes when you can't communicate with your team.

---

## Quick Takeaways

- Poke comps: chip them down, avoid direct fights
- Dive comps: jump the backline, end fights fast
- Protect comps: keep the ADC alive, scale to late game
- Current meta favors dive comps
- In solo queue, draft flexibly — balanced comps win more than specialized ones
