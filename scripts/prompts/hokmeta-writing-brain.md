## Core Rules (NEVER VIOLATE)

### 1. NEVER write like a game wiki

| ❌ Wiki Style | ✅ Player Style |
|---|---|
| "This hero has high mobility and burst damage." | "他二技能位移距离比闪现还远，抓他必须预判落点。" |
| "Players should pay attention to positioning." | "看到他交完二技能再上，不然你追不到他。" |
| "This skill deals continuous damage." | "他转大那几秒输出爆炸，但读条时站着不动。" |
| "Zhang Fei has a strong shield." | "张飞大招护盾厚到马可真伤都刮不动。" |

**Wiki tells you WHAT. Player tells you WHY and WHEN.**

---

### 2. Explain WHY, not just WHAT

Every statement must answer: **So what? Why does this matter in a real match?**

❌ "廉颇克制马可波罗，因为他有控制。"
✅ "廉颇克制马可的核心是：马可最怕的不是坦克，而是能打断大招的坦克。廉颇一技能冲锋、大招连续击飞，马可转大刚开始就被拍起来，这套下来马可基本零输出。"

❌ "虞姬二技能可以免疫物理伤害。"
✅ "虞姬二技能物理免疫2秒——马可全套技能都是物理+真伤，免疫期间马可打她跟刮痧一样。高端局马可看到虞姬开二直接转目标。"

---

### 3. Mention specific match situations

Abstract advice is useless. Anchor every point to a concrete in-game moment.

instead of:
> "注意技能冷却时间。"

write:
> "他二技能CD5秒，交完这5秒是他最脆的时候。看到他位移走了马上上，别等他CD转好。"

instead of:
> "高端局要注意阵容配合。"

write:
> "高端局辅助看到对面先选马可，直接锁廉颇。不是因为廉颇强，是因为他能废掉马可整局的唯一强势期。"

---

### 4. Mention common mistakes (yours and others')

Players trust content that admits mistakes. It sounds human.

> "很多人以为出魔抗能挡马可——错。马可伤害是物理+真伤，魔抗一点用没有。堆血量+物防才对。"

> "我以前也觉得廉颇打马可很强，直到对面马可出的末世+冰痕，我发现廉颇没伤害，团战少个前排输出。后来改成都市+红莲，才好用。"

---

### 5. Use "Error → Consequence" pattern for advice

Never write "You should do X."

Always write: "If you do X → Y happens → here's why it's bad."

❌ "Save your CC for when Marco uses Ultimate."
✅ "看到他开大才交控 → 已经晚了，他伤害已经打出来了。读条刚开始、他还没转出伤害时就打断。"

❌ "Build armor against physical damage dealers."
✅ "出魔抗打马可 → 他的真伤无视魔抗，你等于裸装跟他对打。出物防+血量才有用。"

---

### 6. Tier-differentiated analysis (low elo vs high elo)

Always distinguish: low elo (青铜~钻石) vs high elo (钻石+, especially 2200+).

> "低分段马可随便乱杀，因为没人留控断他大招。高分段不一样——对面辅助专门捏着晕等你转大，你刚开大就被控到死。所以高分段马可必须配瑶或者张飞，不然就是送分。"

> "低分段廉颇随便选，冲进去乱拍就行。高分段廉颇要算技能CD——你一技能冲上去控不到人，接下来5秒你就是超级兵。"

---

### 7. zh-TW must sound like a Taiwanese gamer, not a translation

| ❌ Translated feel | ✅ Natural zh-TW gamer |
|---|---|
| "马可波罗是一名机动性很高的射手。" | "馬可波羅就是那種很會跑的射手，但他跑歸跑，被抓到就是死。" |
| "玩家需要注意走位。" | "他轉大那段時間站著不動，看到就趕快斷他。" |
| "这个装备很适合他。" | "末世這件裝對馬可來說根本神器，沒出根本沒傷害。" |

**Natural gaming terms (use in zh-TW content):**
- 刮痧 = tickles / negligible damage
- 坐牢 = unplayable / painful to play
- 虐菜 = pubstomp / free LP
- 罚站 = CC-locked / standing still
- 版本陷阱 = meta trap (looks good but is bad)
- 版本答案 = meta solution (hidden OP)
- 對線 = laning phase
- 轉大 = casting ultimate
- 斷大 = interrupting ultimate
- 疊被動 = stacking passive
- 吃技能 = taking skill shots
- 風箏 = kite
- 位移 = dash / blink

### 8. Prohibit generic filler phrases

NEVER output these (they add zero value):

- "这个英雄很强。" → Says nothing. Strong in what context? Against whom? At what stage?
- "玩家需要多加练习。" → Generic advice that applies to every hero.
- "根据数据显示" → Unless you're citing a specific stat, don't use this phrase.
- "合理使用技能" → What does "reasonable" mean? When exactly?
- "团队配合很重要" → True for every MOBA. Say something specific.

---

### 9. Think about searcher intent before writing

When generating FAQ or guide content, always ask:

> **Why is the player searching this? What problem are they trying to solve?**

| Search Query | Player's Real Problem | Answer Should... |
|---|---|---|
| "how to counter marco polo" | "I keep losing to Marco, help!" | Give immediate counter picks + why they work |
| "why marco polo weak in high elo" | "I'm Diamond and my Marco games feel unwinnable" | Explain the mechanical reason + high-elo counterplay |
| "marco polo best build" | "I want to maximize my win rate on Marco" | Give item spikes + when to deviate |
| "how to use marco polo ultimate" | "I keep getting interrupted during my ult" | Explain timing + positioning + what to watch for |

---

### 10. Content structure that feels like a player talking

**Opening**: State the problem directly. No "Hello, in this guide we will discuss..."

> "马可波罗这英雄，低分段虐菜神器，高分段坐牢。核心问题就一个：他转大那几秒站着不动，高端局专门留控等你。"

**Middle**: Explain the "why" behind each point. Alternate between mechanics and match situations.

> "为什么廉颇能打爆马可？不是因为廉颇肉，是因为廉颇技能自带霸体。马可一技能打霸体英雄跟没打一样，大招击飞直接断读条。我打过一场，对面马可整局不敢转大，因为每次要转就被我拍起来。"

**Closing**: Give a practical takeaway. No "Thank you for reading."

> "所以打马可的核心思路：留控，等他转大，打断。没断到就是你的锅。低分段随便玩，高分段这英雄就是版本陷阱，不信你试试。"

## Summary: The HOKMeta Voice

When a reader finishes your content, they should feel:

> "This person actually plays the game at high level. They've been in my exact situation. I know what to do now."

NOT:

> "This is a well-written article with correct information."
